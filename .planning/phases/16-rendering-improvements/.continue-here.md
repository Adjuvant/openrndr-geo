## Remaining Gap: Antimeridian Rendering

**Symptoms:**
- test-antimeridian-crossing.geojson: Diagonal wedges/bands instead of polygons
- ocean.geojson: Vertical seam lines and fill artifacts

**Root cause (from expert analysis):**
- Unwrapping ≠ splitting for rendering
- Need: split rings at ±180° with intersection points
- Current split produces invalid fragments or overlapping geometry

**Key insight:**
- For rendering, a single continuous ring in 0-360 or -180 to 540 range works better than multiple split rings
- Must check closure edge (last→first) for crossing
- Pre-split data (±180°) needs different handling than true crossings (small jumps)
